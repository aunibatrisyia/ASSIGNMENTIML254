COMPETITION ACHIEVEMENT 🏅

PERTANDINGAN BAHAS BAHASA MELAYU

Now, I will show you the competitions I have participated in during co-curricular activities. When I was in high school, I was very interested in Malay language debates.
So, I decided to join the debate club in 2019. In 2020, we participated in the school-level debate and competed against the senior team, which consisted of five members. Our team also had five members.
However, due to a lack of experience, our team lost and came in second place.
Then, after several training sessions with expert teachers and exchanging ideas with the seniors, we finally entered a district-level competition the following year. Alhamdulillah, we managed to win gold in this competition.
None of us expected this victory, but this competition somewhat helped elevate our school's name at the district level.

PERTANDINGAN TAEKWONDO PERINGKAT ANTARABANGSA

I also participated in taekwondo competitions at the district and international levels. I have been interested in taekwondo since I was young and have been determined to learn this art of self-defense from Korea.
Honestly, I am an introvert, but my passion for diving into taekwondo and this language has motivated me to step into this co-curricular activity. At the district level, we went to Ipoh and competed in a shopping mall.
As for the international competition, we traveled to the state of Kedah. I gained many experiences from participating in these high-level programs. I hope to continue advancing in whatever I do in the future.
