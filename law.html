ACT IN MALAYSIA REGARDING LIBRARY
There are several acts that a librarian needs to be aware of regarding libraries. Since I am from the library informatics course, this matter is very relevant to my life.
I need to acquire this knowledge to be better prepared for the future. There are many other acts related to the management of libraries and other information materials. So, here is a few acts that I get from my research.

  1. DEPOSIT OF LIBRARY MATERIAL ACT 1986
    An Act to make provision for the collection, conservation, bibliographical control and use of library material published in Malaysia and to provide for matters connected therewith.

  2. INTERPRETATION ACTS 1948 AND 1967
    An Act to provide for the commencement, application, construction, interpretation and operation of written laws; to provide for matters in relation to the exercise of statutory powers and duties; and for matters connected therewith.

  3. NATIONAL LIBRARY ACT 1972
    An Act to provide for the establishment of the National Library and for matters connected therewith.

  4. PRINTING PRESSES AND PUBLICATIONS ACT 1984 (Act 301)
    An Act to regulate the use of printing presses and the printing, importation, production, publishing and distribution of publications and for matters connected therewith.
