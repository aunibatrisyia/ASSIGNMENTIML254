Now, I will talk about my hobby when I have a free time.

I enjoy reading books of any genre, especially those that motivate me to keep moving forward in everything I do. I will read once I have completed other tasks.
I will seek out a comfortable place to focus on my reading.
Sometimes, I will read when I need to wait for something for a long time.
I have a favorite author named Teme Abdullah. He has written about his life, which motivates readers, such as "Pelukis Jalanan" (Street Artist) and "Arkitek Jalanan" (Street Architect).
There are also fiction books like "Empayar" (Empire) and "Kapal Terakhir di Dunia" (The Last Ship in the World).
I really enjoy his writing because it feels like delving deep into the stories he tells. Teme's mature and easy-to-understand writing style really piques my interest in eagerly awaiting his latest works.
Recently, on May 10, 2024, he released a new book titled "Bayang Sofea" (Sofea's Shadow), also a fictional book.

One more thing that intrigues me about Teme is that he never reveals his true face. Every time he posts on Instagram, he blurs his face to conceal his identity, just like the image above.
